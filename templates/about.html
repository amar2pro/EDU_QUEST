About EduQuest

Our Mission

EduQuest is dedicated to creating an inclusive educational ecosystem where every learner, regardless of their abilities or background, can find the perfect learning environment. We believe that education should be accessible to all, and we're committed to breaking down barriers that prevent children from reaching their full potential.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive directory of inclusive schools across Kenya, offering detailed information about accessibility features, specialized programs, and support services. Our platform connects parents, guardians, and students with educational institutions that cater to diverse learning needs.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading platform for inclusive education, fostering a community where every child has equal opportunities to learn, grow, and succeed in an environment that understands and supports their unique needs.
